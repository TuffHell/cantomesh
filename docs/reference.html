一 · 九聲六調 The nine tones, six contours

粵語有六個舒聲調，加上三個收 -p／-t／-k 的入聲，共九聲。平仄之分，全繫於此。 Cantonese has six smooth tones plus three checked (入聲) tones; every 平/仄 call rests on them.

口訣：一、四為平，餘皆仄；凡入聲（收 -p/-t/-k）一律仄。 Rule: tones 1 & 4 are 平 — everything else, and any checked syllable, is 仄.

二 · 聯句格律 The rules of the couplet (梆黃／板腔體)

  • 上句（單數句）收仄聲。 The upper line (odd) ends on an oblique tone.
  • 下句（雙數句）收平聲。 The lower line (even) ends on a level tone.
  • 押韻：各下句的平聲句腳，同屬一韻部。 Rhyme is carried by the level-tone endings of the lower lines, all sharing one rhyme group.

句式模板 Classic 平/仄 line templates

傳統「一三五不論，二四六分明」——單數字位可較寬鬆，重點看雙數字位與句末。 By convention, odd positions are flexible; even positions and the line-ending are what count.
